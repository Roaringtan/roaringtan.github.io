Muhammad Ali

A tribute page to The Greatest

May 25, 1965: Muhammad Ali knocks out challenger Sonny Liston in the first round.

Here are important events in Muhammad's life:

  • January 17, 1942 - Born in Louisville, Kentucky
  • 1954 - Takes up boxing after his bike is stolen
  • 1960 - Wins olypic gold in boxing at the Rome Olypics of 1960
  • 1964 - Muhammad wins the world heavyweight title
  • 1974 - Beats George Foreman in The Rumble in the Jungle
  • 1978 - Becomes a three-time heavyweight champion
  • June 3, 2016 - Dies at age 74 in Scottsdale, Arizona
